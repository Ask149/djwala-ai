Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

What We Collect

When you sign in with Google or Spotify, we receive:

  • Display name and email — to identify your account.
  • Playlist metadata — playlist names and track lists you choose to import. We do not access playlists you don't select.
  • OAuth tokens — stored server-side to access your playlists and (for Spotify Premium) enable playback. Tokens are never exposed to the browser.

How We Use It

  • Playlist import: We read your selected playlist to build a DJ mix from those tracks.
  • Audio analysis: For Spotify tracks, we use Spotify's Audio Features API to get BPM, key, and energy data for harmonic mixing.
  • Playback: If you're a Spotify Premium subscriber, we can play audio through Spotify's Web Playback SDK. Otherwise, audio plays via YouTube.

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We never modify your playlists, library, or account.
  • We never store your Google or Spotify passwords.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics that identify you personally.

Cookies

We use a single HTTP-only session cookie (djwala_session) to keep you logged in. It contains an opaque session ID — no personal data. It expires when you log out or after 30 days.

Data Storage

Your account data (name, email, OAuth tokens) is stored in a SQLite database on our server. Track analysis results are cached to avoid re-processing. No data is stored in the cloud beyond our application server.

Deleting Your Data

You can remove your connected accounts from the user menu at any time. To fully delete your account and all associated data, email djwalaai@gmail.com and we'll remove everything within 7 days.

Using DjwalaAI Without Login

Login is entirely optional. DjwalaAI works fully without an account — just type artist names and get a mix. No data is collected for anonymous users beyond standard server logs (IP address, request URL).

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll note the date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact
